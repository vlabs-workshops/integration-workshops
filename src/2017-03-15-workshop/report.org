* Objectives of the Workshop
  Objectives of the workshop were to -
  + Understand and follow the installation process and usage of the
    Virtual Labs College Cloud Edition.
  + Assist the Nodal Coordinators in conducting workshops using
    Virtual Labs College Cloud Edition.
 

* Expectations after workshop
** Expectations from Participants

     before April review meet.
   + Provide workshop feedback report on Outreach Portal.

     pre/during/post workshops.

** Second Day : March 16, 2017
*** Full day Hands-On training on Installing College Cloud Edition 
    + Partipants were grouped into teams of 4 members each.
    + Each team was given required assets ( a machine, a router, a
      College Cloud Disk, an Installation Manual).
    + A member of each group was requested to take lead and perform
      the installation by following the manual.
    + Lead was given the responsibility to train other members of the
      team and help them in installation.
    + Siva and Raghupathi, frequently helped all groups to solve
      queries and doubts.
    + Every memeber dedicated enough time for installation and perform
      the hands-on.
    + Mr. Ashish from IIT Delhi, talked about the conventions to be
      followed to fill information in Outreach portal.
    + Mr. Ravi Shankar talked about the post workshop expectations.
    + All participants submited feedback form and provided feedback on
      Integration wokshop.
    + Each institute was given a College Cloud disk and were requested
      to return back in April 2017 review meeting.
